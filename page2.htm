Principle Underlying Project Strategy

Frame 15 : Principle of Purposiveness: Projects should have clear objectives to engage students effectively. When students understand the purpose behind their tasks, they approach them with more enthusiasm and commitment. Clear objectives provide direction, helping students stay focused and motivated throughout the project.
Projects are required to have ____________ for achieving students focus and motivation throughout the project .

Answer: clear objective

Frame 16: Principle of Utility:
Projects should be practical and relevant to students’ lives. When students see the usefulness of their work, they are more likely to be interested and engaged. Practical projects not only capture students’ attention but also provide them with skills and knowledge that they can apply in real-world situations.
Practical project are necessary and relevant to students lives as they provide them with ____________ and ______________

Answer:skills , knowledge

Frame 17 : Principle of Reality:
Real-world projects offer authentic learning experiences. By working on tasks that mirror real-life situations, students develop practical skills and problem-solving abilities. Realistic projects also allow students to see the relevance of their learning, leading to deeper understanding and retention of knowledge.
Practical skills and problem solving abilities are developed in students by ______________ experience

Answer:authentic learning

Frame 18 : Principle of Activity:
Activity-centred projects promote hands-on learning. By actively participating in tasks, students not only gain knowledge but also develop practical skills that are essential for success in various fields. Active learning encourages critical thinking, creativity, and collaboration, making it a valuable approach to education.
By active participation in task student develop _____________ required in various fields .

Answer:practical skills

Frame 19 : Principle of Freedom:
Students should have the freedom to choose their projects. When students have autonomy over their learning, they are more invested and motivated to succeed. By selecting projects based on their interests and passions, students take ownership of their education and are more likely to actively engage in the learning process.
Active engagement in the learning process of students can be achieved when they have ____________ over their learning

Answer: autonomy

Frame 20 : Principle of Social Development:
Projects should foster social interaction and collaboration. By working together on meaningful tasks, students develop important social skills such as communication, teamwork, and empathy. Socially engaging projects also help students build relationships, broaden their perspectives, and develop a sense of community responsibility.
By working together on meaningful task student can broaden their perspective by developing important ______________ .

Answer: Social skills
