Merits of Project based learning

Frame 31 : Psychologically Sound:
The project method prioritizes the student’s perspective, focusing on their chosen topics, thereby enhancing motivation and fostering clear goals. It integrates key learning principles and allows flexibility, catering to varying interests and abilities within a supportive social environment.
Project method boosts motivation, sets clear ________ , and encourages ____________ .”

Answer: goals , flexibility

Frame 32 : Practical and Applied Approach:
Emphasizing experiential learning, the project method correlates with real-life situations, addressing students’ daily life challenges. By prioritizing hands-on activities and problem-solving, it ensures practical, applicable learning experiences in social sciences.
Applicable learning experience can be prioritized by ________________ and ________________

Answer: hands -on activities and problem solving

Frame 33 : Integration of Physical and Mental Activities:
Students engage in both manual labour and cognitive tasks during project work, fostering coordination between physical and mental abilities. This approach promotes balanced development and holistic growth in students’ capacities.
Holistic growth in student is fostered by engaging them _______________ _______ and ________________

Answer:manual labour and cognitive tasks

Frame 34: Democratic Teaching Method:
Projects are chosen collaboratively, fostering mutual understanding and cooperation among group members. This democratic approach empowers students with freedom of thought and action, promoting a supportive and inclusive learning environment.
Project in democratic approach are chosen collaboratively so that they can promote _______________ environment

Answer: inclusive

Frame 35 : Development of Social Virtues:
Through collaborative project work, students develop social consciousness and responsibility, learning the value of cooperation and contributing to group goals. This method installs essential virtues for leading a fulfilling social life.
Development of social consciousness and responsibility among students is important for there healthy ___________________

Answer: social life

Frame 36 : Clear and Permanent Knowledge:
Learning by doing encourages active participation, leading to a profound understanding and enduring knowledge. This approach promotes engagement from various angles, fostering deeper insights and ensuring that learning is not just temporary but ingrained in one’s understanding for the long term.
Permanent learning foster ____________ by promoting engagement from various angles .

Answer: deep insight

Frame 37 : Positive Attitude towards Manual Work: Project-based learning not only teaches academic concepts but also nurtures a respect for manual labour. By immersing students in hands-on projects, it highlights the significance of labour in society, fostering a positive attitude towards work and emphasizing its importance for social and economic development.
Project based learning nurtures a respect for manual labour by fostering ___________ towards work

Answer: positive attitude

Frame 38 : Teaching through Correlation:
Projects serve as hubs for holistic learning, connecting theoretical concepts with real-world applications. Through this correlation, students not only acquire knowledge but also understand its relevance in their lives. By integrating curriculum with practical experiences, education becomes more meaningful and adaptable to diverse social and cultural contexts.
Connection of theoretical concepts and real world applications can be achieved through _______________

Answer: project

Frame 39: Scientific Method of Problem Solving: Purposeful activities challenge students to approach problems systematically, nurturing scientific thinking and problem-solving skills. By engaging in structured problem-solving, learners develop the ability to analyse, evaluate, and innovate, preparing them to tackle real-world challenges with confidence and efficacy.
Structured problem solving prepares learner for _____________

Answer: real world challenges

Frame 40: Reduction of Homework Load and Discipline Improvement:
Project-based learning minimizes disciplinary issues by fully engaging students’ mental and physical faculties in meaningful tasks within a democratic and social environment. This approach eliminates the need for excessive homework and promotes constructive self-discipline.
Project based learning highlights the need of __________________ for promoting constructive self discipline

Answer: Social / democratic environment
