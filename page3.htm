Steps of Project based learning method

At times there are spontaneous upsurge of such situation while at others teacher has to plan for the creation of such situations. Students may spot out of a problem while having discussion in the classroom , while working in the laboratory engaging in the extra curricular activities , going in some excursion, or visiting some places of social or general interest .

Frame 24 : Planning of the Project . Effective project implementation necessitates meticulous planning. Students should collaborate with teachers, engage in lively discussions, consult experts, and utilize resources for project planning. Duties and responsibilities, along with financial decisions, must be distributed among students or small groups to ensure successful execution.
Collaborate with teachers necessitates _________________

Answer: meticulous planning

Frame 25 : Execution of the project
Project execution requires collective effort and shared responsibility among all students involved. The planned strategies are put into action, with each member dedicated to success. Challenges are tackled through mutual cooperation under the guidance of an experienced teacher, providing valuable theoretical and practical learning opportunities.
Guidance as an experienced teacher provides _______________ opportunities for students

Answer: mvaluable learning

Frame 26 : Evaluation of the project
Project evaluation is ongoing, involving reviews and assessments of both individual and group efforts periodically. Discussions facilitate open exchange of ideas and self/group evaluations to enhance project execution. Upon completion, a comprehensive assessment is conducted to analyse accomplishments, challenges faced, and lessons learned through constructive discussions.
A comprehensive assessment facilitate ______________________

Answer: constructive discussion

Frame 27 : Recording of the project
Accurate documentation of each project step and related events is crucial. This includes selection, planning, execution, challenges encountered, and achieved results.
Comprehensive recording enables future reference and improvement. Understanding project methodology is facilitated through practical examples demonstrating its application.
Project methodology is facilitated through the demonstration of ________________

Answer: practical examples
