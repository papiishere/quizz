Frame 48 : Limited scalability:
Some projects may be feasible only on a small scale, making it difficult to replicate or scale up for larger classrooms or institutions. This scalability limitation can restrict the widespread adoption of project-based learning across different educational contexts.
Small scale project make it difficult to scale up for ____________________

Answer: larger classroom

Frame 49 : External dependencies:
Projects may rely on external factors such as community involvement, access to experts, or cooperation from outside organizations. Dependence on these external factors introduces uncertainties and logistical challenges that can disrupt project implementation and learning outcomes.
Dependent on external factor causes ___________ and ______________ that disrupt project implementation and learning

Answer: uncertainties , logistical
