Learner centred method

Frame 1 : A learner-centred method prioritizes learner needs, interests, and abilities, engaging students actively, encouraging self-directed exploration, collaboration, and reflection. In this classroom, the teacher facilitates, guiding students to discover knowledge through inquiry-based activities, discussions, and hands-on experiences tailored to individual learning styles.

The method, wherein students are actively engaged and the teacher serves as a guide, is termed “L_ _ _ _ _ _ C_ _ _ _ _ _”

Answer: Lecture method

Frame 2 : Learner-centred methods prioritize active participation and critical thinking. Problem-solving engages learners in real-world challenges. Project-based learning fosters collaboration and creativity through learning by doing. Discovery method empowers independent exploration, while discussion method facilitates interactive dialogue to deepen understanding through diverse perspectives.

Project-based learning fosters collaboration and creativity . It encourages students to engage in _____________, where they actively apply their knowledge and skills to solve real-world challenges

Answer: Learning by doing

Meaning of Project based learning method

Frame 3 : Project-based learning (PBL) is an educational approach where students actively explore real-world challenges and problems through the completion of extended projects. In PBL, students engage in hands-on, inquiry-driven activities that require critical thinking, collaboration, and problem-solving skills. They work on authentic, interdisciplinary projects that often involve research, design, and presentation components.

Working on interdisciplinary project allows students to explore __________________ .

Answer:real world challenges

Frame 4 : PBL encourages student autonomy and ownership of their learning process, as they have the opportunity to make decisions, set goals, and manage their time effectively. Through PBL, students develop not only subject-specific knowledge but also essential skills such as communication, creativity, and resilience, preparing them for success in both academic and real-life contexts.

Project based learning encourages students to develop _____________ knowledge and other essential skill

Answer:to discover

Frame 7 : In PROJECT BASED LEARNING , authenticity ensures that projects mirror real-life situations, bridging classroom learning with practical applications. This approach boosts students’ motivation and engagement by showing how their learning directly relates to their everyday lives.
Authenticity ensures projects replicate__________, linking __________ .

Answer: reality, theory

Frame 8 : Collaboration lies at the heart of PROJECT BASED LEARNING , where students team up to tackle complex challenges or complete projects together. This fosters skills like communication, teamwork, and cooperation, mirroring how people work together in professional settings.
PBL emphasizes_____________, fostering _____________and_____________, echoing professional dynamics

Answer:collaboration, teamwork, communication

Frame 9 : Inquiry , PROJECT BASED LEARNING encourages students to explore topics actively, ask questions, conduct research, and find solutions on their own. This nurtures critical thinking as students analyse information, assess evidence, and draw conclusions to solve real-world problems.
Project Based Learning fosters independent exploration and____________ , catalysing _______________ thinking to navigate complex challenges effectively.

Answer: problem-solving, critical

Frame 10 : In PROJECT BASED LEARNING , students take charge of their learning by actively participating in planning, executing, and evaluating projects. They set goals, manage time, make decisions, and reflect on progress, promoting independence, self-direction, and accountability for their learning journey.
PROJECT BASED LEARNING empowers students to lead __________ ,__________ and _____________their projects.

Answer: plan, execute, and evaluate
